1. Current-state discovery and dependency mapping
2. Workload assessment and cloud readiness analysis
3. Migration strategy selection: retire, retain, rehost, replatform, refactor, rearchitect, rebuild, or replace
4. Landing zone, governance, identity, security, and networking readiness
5. Workload-specific migration design
6. Cost, licensing, sizing, and performance validation
7. Migration wave planning and cutover runbook design
8. Backup, DR, rollback, and business continuity planning
9. Migration execution oversight and validation
10. Post-migration optimization and operational handoff
